Modern Data Platforms for Operationalizing Generative AI at Scale

GenAI is currently evolving faster than most organizations can adapt. From AI copilots and chatbots to custom LLMs and autonomous agents, the use cases are exploding. At the same time, most data platforms were not built for GenAI — they were built to power dashboards and batch analytics.

The table stakes have shifted dramatically. Operationalizing GenAI at scale requires the ability to manage large volumes of unstructured data, low-latency inference, real-time context delivery, retrieval- augmented generation (RAG), and prompt orchestration — just to name a few. If your infrastructure cannot support these types of workloads reliably and at scale, your GenAI investments will stall.
